# Source basis and realism notes

The fixtures are synthetic, but their scenarios are modeled after recurring Romanian scam patterns documented by public safety sources and community reports.

- ARB - fraud typologies, WhatsApp fraud, spoofing, credit/BNR/ATM crypto: https://www.arb.ro/ce-trebuie-sa-stim-pentru-a-nu-deveni-victime-ale-fraudei-bancare-ramona-rusu-presedintele-comisiei-antifrauda-arb-tipologiile-de-frauda-sunt-intr-o-dinamica-permanenta-iar-metodele-de-fr/

## Community-inspired pattern discovery

Reddit/community cases were used only as scenario inspiration and for test diversity, not as high-confidence blocklist sources. No full real user messages were copied.

Examples include:

- metoda accidentul targeting grandparents;
- context-real courier scam after actual eMAG/support activity;
- spoofed Police/BNR/bank calls with WhatsApp documents;
- WhatsApp new-number/broken-phone messages.

## Copyright/trademark note

Brand names are used nominatively to test impersonation and official-domain logic. No real logos or proprietary email templates were copied. All scam content is synthetic.
